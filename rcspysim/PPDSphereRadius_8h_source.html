39 /**
40  * Descriptor for the radius of a sphere-shaped body.
41  *
42  * The sphere must be the first shape of the body for this to work.
43  * This is specific to the ball-on-plate task since it also has to adjust
44  * the ball's position to prevent it from clipping through the plate.
45  *
46  * Note that this does not update the inertia based on the shape changes,
47  * for that, add PPDMassProperties after this descriptor.
48  *
49  * Exposes:
50  *
51  * - radius:
52  * Radius of the sphere [m]
53  */

57  /**

63  * Use "" if the sphere is defined in world coordinates
64  */
